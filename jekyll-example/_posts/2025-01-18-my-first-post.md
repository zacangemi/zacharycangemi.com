# The Current State of AGI Predictions

Everyone seems to have an opinion on when AGI will arrive. From Sam Altman's cautious optimism to Yann LeCun's skepticism, the timeline predictions vary wildly.

## What I'm Watching

After years in the trenches of data science and financial modeling, here's what I think actually matters:

- **Hardware scaling**: The compute requirements are still massive
- **Data quality**: More data ≠ better models necessarily  
- **Emergent capabilities**: The surprising behaviors we can't predict

## My Take

The path to AGI isn't just about scaling transformers. It's about understanding how intelligence emerges from simple rules - something I think about constantly when analyzing complex financial systems.

Brain emulation might actually beat us there. Why? Because we have an existence proof: the human brain works.
